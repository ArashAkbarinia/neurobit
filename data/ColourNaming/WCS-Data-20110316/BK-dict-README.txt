The file "BK-dict.txt" contains information on the color terms 
appearing in the naming data for all 20 languages 
in Appendix 1 of _Basic Color Terms: Their Universality 
and Evolution_, Brent Berlin and Paul Kay (Berkeley: UC Press, 
1969 [1991]; ISBN: 0-520-07635-4).

The lines in this file are formatted as follows:

lnum \t abbr \t tnum \t term \n

where the field abbreviaitions are as follows:

lnum	:	language number (as in ""BK-term.txt"")
abbr	:	term abbreviation
tnum	:	term number (sequential numbering of BK terms per lnum)
term	:	an ASCII transcription of the BK transcription
